About the Show

Dinner and a Show is a weekly podcast where four friends gather around the table to serve up bold opinions, hilarious stories, and unforgettable conversations. Every episode brings a fresh mix of pop culture, personal anecdotes, and unfiltered takes that keep listeners coming back for seconds. Whether you're commuting, cooking, or just chilling, pull up a chair — there's always room at our table.
